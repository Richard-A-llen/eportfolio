Reflections on the teamwork

The stand out experience so far on the journey which is this course. Great team - diverse, interesting and intelligent. There was a mix of abilities regarding coding with me being one of the least comfortable which I believe lead to me leaning on others at times during the development process. I was able to demonstrate the code for parametrised queries, I took a front role for the code demonstration presentation and I designed the ReadMe file and did most of the coding for it in MarkDown. I should have contributed more to design of the system architecture and got more involved in the implementation of flask and how to connect the modules. Wang was an excellent and patient teammate who helped us through the most challenging sections of the coding process. I was very relieved but also proud of our teams project and I feel more confident to take part in teamwork in the future.
